40%

of food produced in the US goes uneaten

but...

Most food thrown out from grocery stores is still perfectly edible! Sometimes an avocado in a bag has been spoiled and the whole bag goes. Occasionally produce pack sizes are just too big to sell everything. And confusing sell-by dates turn consumers off from buying older items.

How it works

Stores list food items that may be partially damaged, ugly, or close approaching the sell by dates at severely discounted rates

You favorite your closest stores, and we give you a list of great food available at those stores right now

Order online, and pick up your food items in the store. Cook an awesome meal, and be sure to tell us all about it!

Sound like a win-win-win?

We think so! We're hard at work getting everything lined up to make sure you have access to the best food at super low prices. If you're interested, sign up for our mailing list to stay up to date.

Are you a grocery store, restaurant, or small business looking to go to zero waste? We're super excited to start working together - reach out to us at contact@sustayn.io and let's talk!
